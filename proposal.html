I thought I could love you for twelve days —
January to December, each month a chapter of my heart.

Then I thought, maybe just six —
Spring's bloom, Summer's warmth, Autumn's gold, Winter's whisper,
Late Autumn’s longing, Rain’s tender kiss.

But no, perhaps only three —
Yesterday’s dreams, Today’s breath, Tomorrow’s hope.

Or maybe just two —
Day and Night, endless in their dance.

Maybe, Oneday
Ah, but my heart knows the truth —
I can love you for only one day...
Forever.
